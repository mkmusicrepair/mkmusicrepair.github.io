About Us

We are a full service band instrument repair shop that specializes in the repair, maintenance and restoration of brass, woodwind and percussion instruments. Started as Mark Kuklok Band Instrument Repair, we have been providing schools, teachers and students with convenient, professional, and affordable repair for 20+ years.

We are located in downtown Owatonna, Minnesota in the lower level of Tone Music.

We also do repair for Waseca Music.

During the school year we offer free weekly pick-up and drop-off of instruments at area schools. Turn-around time is usually 1 week. Please click on the Services tab for more information.

C Trumpet Conversion

One of the services offered at MK Music Repair is converting a Bb trumpet into a C trumpet. This is not a very large part of the business but it is something that we have been doing off and on for the last 17 years.

We start with an Olds Ambassador Bb trumpet, strip the lacquer, remove the bell, leadpipe, and most of the outer slide tubes. Then we cut tubes to the proper length and rebuild the instrument from the valve casing on up. The leadpipe is replaced with a Bach Strad 25 Bb leadpipe and the tuning slide is reversed. The 1st slide has a saddle installed and the 3rd slide has a fixed ring attached. Both the 1st & 3rd slides are properly fitted for smooth and quick action. The Olds leadpipe/bell braces are discarded and replaced with typical leadpipe/bell to casing braces and "S" braces between the bell and leadpipe. Your choice of conventional or Amado waterkeys are installed. Usually we leave a raw brass with a polished or brushed finish but other finishes are available at an additional cost.

This process is not simply cutting a few slides. We completely disassemble the instrument and rebuild it from the ground up. The completed instrument bears very little resemblance to the original trumpet. We have done about 8 of these conversions and all the owners have been very happy with the way the trumpet plays and the intonation.

It usually takes anywhere from 2 to 4 months to complete a C trumpet conversion depending on the season.
