About Us

We are a full service band instrument repair shop that specializes in the repair, maintenance and restoration of brass, woodwind and percussion instruments. Started as Mark Kuklok Band Instrument Repair, we have been providing schools, teachers and students with convenient, professional, and affordable repair for over 18 years.

We are located in downtown Owatonna, Minnesota in the lower level of Tone Music (www.tonemusicmn.com).

During the school year we offer weekly pick-up and drop-off of instruments at area schools. Turn-around time is usually 1 week. Please click on the Services tab for more information.

Note: We do not have the staff or expertise to repair string or electronic instruments including guitars.

What's New

We recently purchased a brand new Ultrasonic Cleaner for the shop. It is extremely thorough, and considerably faster, too! It's also better for the environment as it doesn't require any harmful chemicals to operate.

You'll be pleasantly surprised with how clean your instruments will come out!

Road Route

For the convenience of our customers, Mark travels two days a week during the school year to pick up and drop off instruments at numerous Southern Minnesota and Suburban schools. The road routes are listed below. If you have an instrument that you would like picked up at one of the schools please contact us by phone or email. Turn-around is usually one week for road route instruments. You will be contacted with an estimate before any work is started.

Our Amazing Team

Mark Kuklok

Owner

Mark has been a full time repair technician since 1992. He is a graduate of the Band Instrument Repair Course at Redwing Technical College (now Southeast Technical College) and has a Bachelor of Science degree in Music Education from North Dakota State University. Mark started his instrument repair career in Mankato, MN and started MK Music Repair in Owatonna in 2000.
